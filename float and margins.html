The Floating Effect

This is the paragraph text. Because the image has "float: left" applied to it, this text initially sits to the right side of the image. The "margin-right" we added ensures there is a nice gap between this text and the blue image block.

Here is where the wrapping happens: As we add more and more text, it eventually runs out of vertical space next to the image. Because of the normal document flow, the text will naturally wrap around and continue on the lines directly underneath the image, spanning the full width of the container. This demonstrates how the float sits within the content flow rather than being completely removed from it.
